Revenue lost from attrition

To calculate revenue lost from attrition: consider client who purchase in last year, but not purchase in this year as lost clients. in this year, assume lost client purchase the same amount as last year For year 2015, we can't calculate revenue lost from attrition, for we don't know the data in 2014. For year 2016, lost customers are customers who purchased in 2015 but didn't purchase in 2016. We calculate the revenue for lost customers in 2015. For year 2017, lost customers are customers who purchased in 2016 but didn't purchase in 2017. We calculate the revenue for lost customers in 2016.
